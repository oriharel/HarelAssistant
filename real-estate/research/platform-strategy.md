## Bump Strategy

### Yad2
- Paid bump service, can bump **every 4 hours minimum**
- Price update (even small) refreshes listing in algorithm
- Video in listing recommended by Yad2
- Use [ידאטה](https://yadata.yad2.co.il) valuation tool — affects listing display
- **Best times:** Sun-Tue 09:00-11:00, Thu 18:00-20:00

### Madlan
- Free basic listing
- Madlan sends **automatic email every 1-2 weeks** to refresh/update/delete
- Always click "bump" when email arrives
- Update description or photo order to trigger algorithm refresh
- Ensure exact address including building number (affects mapping)
- **Weekly refresh sufficient**

### Facebook
- Refresh every 5-7 days with different text/photo
- Same post gets less exposure — vary content each time
- Comment on own post to bump in feed
- **Best times:** Sun-Wed, 20:00-22:00
